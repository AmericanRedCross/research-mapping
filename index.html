Research conducted by the global Red Cross Red Crescent network

Advancing the humanitarian mission through evidence and learning

The Red Cross and Red Crescent Research Consortium is made up of a disparate but like minded group of research oriented organizations representing different parts of the Red Cross/Red Crescent Network. This map shows that as whole we are not only an extensive and rich data producer but also a center of gravity for research and knowledge development. This map is a collection of current research activities, studies and academic papers that are supported by the members of the Research Consortium and captures some of the research activities which, when considered as a whole, would rival most universities, academic and research institutions.

This commitment to expanding an evidence base, investing in new knowledge and sharing learning across our network is a valuable and valued contribution to our network and the wider humanitarian community.
